Pricing and Package Options

What Packages are available?

We offer a wide range of packages for the different needs of every group. We offer packages for day trips, overnight stays, hikes and we have dining options with every package. We are sure that you will be able to find the right deal for your group that meets all your needs at a competative price.

Day Trips

Enjoy a single day out on the water rafting and experiencing nature. This package includes reaft rental, guides, and shuttle back to start. This package is only $40 a person*.

Overnight Trips

Want to stay in nature longer than a day? Overnight adventures are offered for just $50 dollars a person* per day. This package includes everything in the day package plus camping equipment rental for the night stays. Available up to 3 nights.

Dining Options

Here at Dry Oar we want you to be able to enjoy the journey the whole way and to assist you in enjoying your adventure we do offer meals cooked by the guides for your group. Day trips have a lunch option for $10 per person and ovenight trips will provide all meals for $25 per person* per day.

* Minimum 6 people per group
