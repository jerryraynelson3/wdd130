Site Plan

Jerry Ray Nelson III

Site name will be Dry Oar Rafting and the proposed domain name is dryoar.com
The purpose of this site is to allow propective patrons of my white water rafting experience to make and change appointments, learn about the different water trails available, and learn about saftey guidlines that we have in place to allow them to enjoy their rafting experience.
The target audience for this would be retirement communities who want a fun activity for their residents.
